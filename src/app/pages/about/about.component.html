About Our Cactus Webshop

Our Story

Welcome to our cactus webshop, where passion meets cultivation. With over 50 years of dedicated expertise, we take pride in offering a curated collection of cacti grown from seeds collected from our extensive and cherished cactus collection.

Our founder, a seasoned cactus enthusiast, has been nurturing these extraordinary plants with love and care. The seeds themselves have been meticulously collected over the years, each carrying a unique story and history from various corners of the world.

We strive to share the beauty and diversity of cacti with enthusiasts worldwide. Every cactus you find here has a rich heritage, grown by our team with passion and a deep connection to these fascinating desert plants.

Our Founder

Meet the visionary behind our cactus webshop. With more than five decades of experience, our founder has not only cultivated an impressive collection of cacti but has also become a renowned figure in the cactus community.

The dedication and love for cacti have been the driving force behind the success of our webshop. Our founder's commitment to offering high-quality, home-grown cacti is reflected in every plant we sell.

Join us on this journey as we continue to share the joy of collecting and growing cacti with fellow enthusiasts around the globe.
